The Pioneer Forest

Hey Pioneers! Join The Pioneer Forest to grow a greener world with your Pi! 🌱 Through Tree-Nation, your donated Pi will plant trees via reforestation projects under my Tree-Nation profile. Every contribution restores ecosystems and supports local communities!

🌍 Tree prices vary by project, influenced by local labor, equipment, tree species, project scale, and maintenance. Some projects are more cost-effective, and small donations may be combined to fund a full tree when needed. Every Pi makes a difference! 🌳

This is a community-powered campaign with no set end date, shaped by fellow Pioneers’ votes. It’s private and trust-based—while I aim to process donations promptly, delays might occur due to health, travel, or life events. Thanks for your understanding! Check the projects page for details on supported initiatives.

Go Pi. Go Green. Go The Pioneer Forest. 💚
