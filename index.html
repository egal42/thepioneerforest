The Pioneer Forest

A community-driven reforestation initiative within the Pi Network ecosystem.

The Pioneer Forest supports documented tree planting and CO₂ offset activity through independent environmental initiatives.

Contributions, records, and progress are shared publicly to provide transparency and long-term continuity.

What grows here may take different forms over time: living records, quiet presences, and shared artifacts shaped by the same principles.

The forest remains open.
So does the path forward.
