Preservation Mt. Elgon – Uganda

The Guardian of Growth Campaign supports reforestation through Tree-Nation’s Preservation Mt. Elgon project in Uganda.

This initiative not only combats climate change but empowers local communities — especially women. Of 24 community-run nursery sites, 13 are led by women, showcasing how environmental work and gender equality can grow together.

With over 295,000 trees already funded, this project is restoring ecosystems, improving soil health, and providing livelihoods in a region deeply affected by deforestation.

Guardians of Growth proudly contribute to this effort — planting hope, restoring balance, and proving that even a single Pi can change the world 🌍.
