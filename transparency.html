THE PIONEER FOREST

Transparency & Scope

Structure, boundaries, and how the project operates.

The Pioneer Forest is a community-driven reforestation initiative operating within the Pi Network ecosystem.

It is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Pi Core Team.

The Pioneer Forest focuses on documented environmental impact, transparency of activity, and long-term continuity of public records.

How Impact Is Created

Contributions to The Pioneer Forest are used to fund tree planting and CO₂ offset projects through independent environmental providers.

Project selection depends on availability, impact potential, geographic diversity, and long-term sustainability.

Reforestation activity is currently carried out through platforms such as Tree-Nation.

Documentation & Public Record

The Pioneer Forest maintains a public record of activity through multiple channels:

  • The Impact page on this site
  • Updates on the Fireside #ThePioneerForest channel
  • External planting certificates where available
  • Public blockchain transaction records

This layered approach allows independent verification of contributions and outcomes.

Funding Model

The Pioneer Forest operates primarily through voluntary Pi contributions.

Contributions are not investments and do not grant ownership, financial returns, or decision-making rights.

Allocation of funds prioritizes environmental impact and project continuity.

What The Pioneer Forest Does Not Provide

  • No ownership of trees or land
  • No investment or financial product
  • No guaranteed returns
  • No legal claim over planted sites

Independence

The Pioneer Forest does not operate its own planting infrastructure.

Instead, it relies on established third-party organizations that specialize in environmental projects.

This approach allows focus on coordination, transparency, and community engagement.

Verification

Pi transactions can be verified through the Pi Block Explorer .

External planting activity can be reviewed through the Tree-Nation profile .

Contact
