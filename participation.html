THE PIONEER FOREST

Participation

Ways to take part in The Pioneer Forest beyond a direct donation.

The Pioneer Forest is a community-driven initiative within the Pi Network ecosystem.

Participation in The Pioneer Forest can take different forms. Some paths support direct reforestation impact. Others support visibility, continuity, or shared presence within the wider forest.

This page gathers those paths in one place.

Important: Full functionality for Pi-related transactions, marketplace access, and partner services is available within the Pi Browser.

Shared Participation

The Pioneer Forest includes shared digital artifacts and related participation paths that connect symbolic record with real-world impact.

These are not ownership claims, personal tree titles, or financial assets. Their purpose is documentary, symbolic, and communal.

Ways to Participate

Direct Impact

Support through donation

The most direct path is a Pi contribution through the official wallet of The Pioneer Forest. This funds documented reforestation activity and public impact records.

How to Support →

Shared Artifacts

Take part through digital editions

Some editions or shared products connected to The Pioneer Forest may be made available through external marketplace partners.

These items record shared participation in documented impact, rather than ownership of trees, land, or environmental assets.

Public Record

Follow and engage on Fireside

The Fireside channel serves as the live public record of milestones, new plantings, updates, and structural changes within The Pioneer Forest.

Fire, replies, and thoughtful engagement help increase visibility for The Pioneer Forest and its documented outcomes.

Visit the Fireside channel →

Forest Presence

Explore branch pages and linked records

Some forms of participation are expressed through branch pages within The Pioneer Forest, such as Elowen and earlier chapters like Guardians of Growth.

These pages help preserve continuity, narrative, and visible history.

Enter the Forest →

Marketplace Use

When shared products or editions are offered, they may be distributed through independent marketplace partners.

These platforms provide their own listing, purchase, and account systems. The Pioneer Forest remains responsible for the meaning, documentation, and real-world impact associated with the items it publishes.

For marketplace-based participation, Pi Browser use is recommended as the primary environment for full access and compatibility.

What Participation Is Not

  • Not ownership of trees or land
  • Not a financial investment
  • Not a promise of future returns
